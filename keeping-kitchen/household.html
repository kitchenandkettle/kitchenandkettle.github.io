On cleaning products

You don't need a different product for every surface. Most of what a house needs can be handled with soap, an acid (vinegar or citric acid), and an abrasive (baking soda or a good scrubber). The rest is knowing when to use each one — and when to admit a commercial product does the job better. No absolutism. Just less.
