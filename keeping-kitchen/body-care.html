An honest note

Some things are genuinely worth making yourself. Salves, lip balm, body oil, and herbal teas are easy, cheap, and better than most store-bought versions. Soap is satisfying but takes practice. Lotion is finicky. Sunscreen should be bought from people with laboratories. This section tells you which is which, honestly.
