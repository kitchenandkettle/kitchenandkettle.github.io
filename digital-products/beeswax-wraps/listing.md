## Description (copy-paste)
Plastic-free food wrap made at home. Three ingredients, an oven, and 20 minutes.

Beeswax wraps are pieces of cotton infused with beeswax, pine resin, and jojoba oil — a natural, reusable alternative to plastic wrap for covering bowls, wrapping cheese, and folding around sandwiches. Washable, reusable for up to a year, and compostable at the end.

**What's inside (5 pages):**
- What beeswax wraps are and what they replace
- Complete ingredients list with why each one matters
- Step-by-step oven method — no sewing, no special tools
- How to use, wash, store, and re-coat your wraps
- Honest note — what beeswax wraps won't do (and what they will)

**The philosophy:** One batch, one afternoon, one less roll of plastic in the landfill.
