## Description (copy-paste)
Not medical advice — just a record book.

Learn how to make tea, salves, tinctures, and infused oils from the plants around you. Then write down what worked. This journal teaches the basic methods — how to extract, infuse, and preserve — and gives you space to record your own experiments. No dosage charts, no medical claims. Just the techniques and a place to keep notes.

**What's inside (17 pages):**
- Your family's needs — start with who you're making things for and why
- Herb journal — 8 plants with space to study each one
- How to make tea — hot and cold infusion methods with tea blend journal
- How to make salves — infused oil to beeswax, step by step with recipe journal
- How to make tinctures — the folk method, no math required, with tincture records
- How to make infused oils — cold and warm methods with oil journal
- Drying & storing herbs — when to harvest, how to dry, how to store, with drying log
- Herb inventory — track what you have on hand (10 entries)
- Harvest logs — what you picked, when, and where (8 entries)
- Drying records — track each batch from fresh to finished (8 entries)
- Notes — four full-page spaces for whatever doesn't fit a category
