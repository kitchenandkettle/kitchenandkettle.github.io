How to Use This Journal

This is not a guide to herbal medicine. It's a blank record book — a place to track what you grow, harvest, dry, and make in your own kitchen. No dosage charts, no treatment recommendations. Just pages for your notes.

Fill in what matters to you. Skip what doesn't. The journal doesn't judge. If you're new to herbs, start with the harvest log and tea blends pages — those are the hardest to remember and the most rewarding to look back on.

What's Inside

  • Herb inventory — what you have on hand, when you got it, where it came from
  • Harvest logs — what you picked, when, where, and how much
  • Drying records — start to finish, method and yield
  • Salve recipes — ingredients, method, date made, notes on the batch
  • Tea blends — what went in, proportions, how it tasted
  • Tincture records — herb, menstruum, ratios, start and decant dates
  • Notes — blank space for whatever doesn't fit a category

Type directly into this journal — your entries save to your device automatically. No account needed. Clearing your browser data clears your entries.
