## Description (copy-paste)
Five practical templates for running a kitchen that holds up under real life — no perfectionism, no aspirational photos, no pressure.

This isn't a rigid routine. It's a set of tools that work together. Use the ones you need. Skip the rest. The goal is fewer decisions, not more paperwork.

**What's inside:**
- **Weekly Meal Planner** — Decide what to eat. Reduces the 5pm "what's for dinner" panic
- **Grocery List** — Organized by store section so you move through the store once
- **Pantry Inventory** — Know what you have. Prevents buying your third jar of cumin
- **Freezer Inventory** — Know what's in there. Prevents mystery containers of ice
- **Weekly Prep Checklist** — The few things that make the week smoother if you do them ahead
- Notes pages, tips throughout, and a "how to use this system" guide

**Two formats included:**
- **Interactive HTML** — Type directly into the templates. Your entries save automatically to your device (local storage — no account, no cloud, fully private). Note: clearing your browser cookies or history will clear your saved entries.
- **Printable PDF** — Print blank copies and fill them out by hand. Print as many as you need.

**This is not:** a wellness brand or a lifestyle aspiration. It's a tool for feeding yourself and the people you live with.

▶ This is a digital download. No physical product will be shipped.
